Charity Information

Proceeds

100% of all charitable donations are distributed between shelters that are on our registry

Subscriptions

You can become a monthly subcriber with a one time payment every month of either $5, $10, or $20 dollars. With a subscription, the shelters your donations help will send you updates every month via SMS or through post.

Why Choose Coins4Cats?

Coins4Cats is the worlds leading charity for animal shelters around the world. Just last year, our program helped over 750 animal shelters provide better living conditions for our furry friends.
